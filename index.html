Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my preferences?

You can reset all your preferences by going to Settings > Reset Options. This will restore Trevor AI to its default state.

Is my conversation data private?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. Conversations are encrypted and you can choose not to save your chat history in Settings.

Can I use Trevor AI for commercial purposes?

Yes, with our Pro plan you can use Trevor AI for business applications. Check our pricing page for more details.

How often is the knowledge base updated?

Trevor AI receives weekly updates to its knowledge base and monthly major version updates with new features.
